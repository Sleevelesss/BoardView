Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2025

BoardView allows you to view your own Pinterest boards and pins. We do not store or share your data; access tokens are kept server-side and automatically purged 30 days after issuance.

Data We Collect

  • Access Tokens
    Stored server-side only to authenticate API calls. Not shared, sold, or exposed to third parties.
  • User Data
    We fetch your boards and pins from Pinterest only after you authorize the app. We do not store this data persistently—data is held in session memory and cleared on logout or session expiry.

Security

All data transmissions occur over HTTPS. Access tokens are encrypted at rest and never stored in client-side code or cookies.

Use Cases

  • Mobile-Friendly Browsing
    Quickly scroll through your own boards and pins on any device with a responsive, lightweight UI.
  • Offline Inspiration
    Browse and bookmark ideas (recipes, travel, DIY) without navigating Pinterest’s heavier interface.
  • Content Consolidation
    Search and filter across all your boards in one place to find specific pins faster.
  • Safe, Read-Only Access
    We only request boards:read and pins:read scopes, so no content posting or account changes.
  • Team Sharing
    Project your BoardView dashboard in meetings or workshops to share inspiration in real time.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or your data, please email us at questslayer99@gmail.com.
